--- title: Montreal All-Girl Hack Night layout: page ---

November 26, 2013

@Shopify Montreal

Mahtab Ghamsari and Stefanie Schirmer will talk about music and technology. They'll do a runthrough of music technology history and popular tools with two live demos at the end. The software covered in the demos are: Max/MSP (or PureData the open-source version) and Overtone.

In this session, we'll make a simple sine wave in puredata as an exercise. If you'd like to follow along, download PD-extended and try to see if you can launch it from the unix terminal.

You should come if:

you're a woman & you like writing code &
you want to write code around / hang out with other women who write code
