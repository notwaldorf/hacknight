Hack Night

January 22, 2013

6:30PM - 9PM

@ Notman House


Register on Eventbrite

You should come if:

you're a woman & you like writing code &
you want to write code around / hang out with other women who write code

We're female developers in Montreal. We decided that Montreal needs another event for female developers to hang out, talk technology, and hack on cool projects.

Bring yourself, your vexing technical problems, and your expertise! If you feel like coding, bring a laptop. We provide internet and witty banter.

If you're interested in giving a talk at a future meetup or have any other suggestions, questions, or photos of cats you'd like to share, email us. You can also follow us on twitter for updates.

Organizers: Julia & Monica

FAQ

  • What if I don't have anything I'm programming? This sounds intimidating / I don't code on Wednesdays / my laptop weighs a megaton.
    Just come and hang out, then. Leave your laptop at home if you want! We want to meet you! You yes you!
  • Is there internet?
    Yes! Internet is nerd food and we will have internet for you to hack on your things that need hacking.
  • Is there food?
    Yes! Because they're awesome, Shopify has offered to sponsor us. We will feed you beer, pizza, healthy snacks and juice! There will be vegetarian food but not vegan or gluten-free options. Email us if this is a problem for you and we'll see what we can do in future events.
  • Can I bring kids?
    We don't have any ability to provide childcare, so it's best if you don't. If this is stopping you from coming, let us know!
  • Is the space wheelchair-accessible?
    Notman house is not wheelchair-accessible :(. Let us know if this is a problem for you!

    Yes! Here it is.
