  • What if I don't have anything I'm programming? This sounds intimidating / I don't code on Wednesdays / my laptop weighs a megaton.
    Just come and hang out, then. Leave your laptop at home if you want! We want to meet you! You yes you!
  • I can't make it this time! How can I find out about the next one?
    Follow us on Twitter or join our mailing list
  • I don't have any projects I'm working on, but I'd like to
    We have ideas for you!
  • Can men come?
    Only female-identified folks for now :)
  • Is there internet?
    Yes! Internet is nerd food and we will have internet for you to hack on your things that need hacking.
  • Is there food?
    Yes! We will feed you beer, healthy snacks and juice!
  • Can I bring kids?
    We don't have any ability to provide childcare, so it's best if you don't. If this is stopping you from coming, let us know!
  • Is the space wheelchair-accessible?
    Notman house is not wheelchair-accessible :(. Let us know if this is a problem for you! Future events might be.
