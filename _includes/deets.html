Tanya Short will introduce the basics of making a 3d game in the Unity game engine, including the basics of physics, controls, lighting, textures, and particle effects. If you bring a laptop with Unity installed, then at the end you'll have a little car awkwardly driving around that you can take home.

Tanya is the creative director of Kitfox Games, an independent games company in Montreal, and she's been making games for over seven years. She also co-founded Pixelles Montreal, an amazing program that helps women get started with creating games.
